Rushing Theta Tau

Thinking about rushing Theta Tau?

Theta Tau is University of Michigan's only Professional Engineering Fraternity. Our purpose is the develop and maintain a high standard of professional interest amoung its members and unite them in a strong bond of fraternal fellowship.

Founded over 15 years ago in 1994, Theta Gamma Chapter is a group of people who are well rounded, come from all Engineering majors, and are eager to meet you!
